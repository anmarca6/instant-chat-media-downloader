# Privacy Policy - Instant Chat Media Downloader

**Last updated:** January 25, 2026

## Overview

Instant Chat Media Downloader is a Chrome extension that helps you download images and documents from WhatsApp Web conversations. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect (Optional - Opt-in Only)

If you choose to enable anonymous usage statistics, we collect:

- **Event type**: Whether you performed a "Magic Scan" or "Full Scan"
- **File count**: Number of files found during a scan
- **Date**: Aggregated daily (YYYY-MM-DD format only)

This data is completely anonymous and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users.

### What We DO NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No chat content or messages
- No file names, URLs, or file content
- No contact information
- No browsing history
- No user identifiers or device information
- No cookies or tracking

## Data Usage

The anonymous statistics we collect (if you opt-in) are used solely to:

- Understand how the extension is being used
- Identify which features are most valuable
- Improve the extension's functionality

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

## Your Choices

- **Opt-in**: Analytics are disabled by default. You must explicitly consent to share statistics.
- **Opt-out anytime**: You can disable analytics at any time in Advanced Options.
- **No impact**: Declining analytics does not affect the extension's functionality.

## Data Storage

Anonymous statistics are stored on secure servers and retained for product improvement purposes only.

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage only to remember:
- Your analytics consent preference
- No other data is stored locally

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
